What's the difference between roles?
Staff: Can view inventory, create orders, and receive goods
Admin: Can manage suppliers, customers, and approve orders
Superadmin: Full system access including user management
My account is locked. What do I do?
After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 15 minutes for security. Wait 15 minutes or contact your administrator.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click the "Forgot Password?" link above the Sign In button. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.
Is my data secure?
Yes! Janstro IMS uses industry-standard security including password hashing, JWT authentication, rate limiting, and audit logging. All sensitive data is encrypted.
Can I access this from mobile devices?
Yes! Janstro IMS is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
